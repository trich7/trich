Tute Genomics announces Open Access Program for the Academic and Non-Profit research communities

What is the Tute Open Access Program?

Tute Genomics is helping to accelerate gene discovery efforts by offering up it's best-in-class technology for free in academic and nonprofit research settings. This new program supports the genetics research community by providing free access to the Tute Genomics genome interpretation platform during the 2014 calendar year.

Why is it free?

We want to introduce the "Tute Genomics Software", powered by ANNOVAR, to the Academic Research community. Tute Genomics wants to support the acceleration of scientific discovery.

Who can join the Tute Open Access Program?

You must be an academic or nonprofit organization. Use your .edu or .org email to sign up. Once the form is filled out and submitted the Tute Open Access committee will alert you when your account is accessible. Sign up now and join the Tute Open Access program.
