1. First click deal to deal the cards.

2. Then click play to play each round.

3. The highest card wins the pair.

4. If there is a tie, war is declared. 3 cards are mucked and the following fourth card decides the winner of the war. The winner takes all cards.

5. Aces have the value of 1 and are the lowest card.

6. The first player to win the entire deck of cards is the winner.
