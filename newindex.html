About T.Rich Design

T.Rich Design was started by Tyler Richards back in 2005, when Tyler first created designs and slapped them on to t-shirts. People like them enough that Tyler thought to do more than just t-shirts. T.Rich design brings creativity, confidence, and straight swagger to any project. T.Rich Design makes websites, but we don't stop there - from websites to print materials, or ads to graphic design. Everything is crafted with modern design techniques and attention to detail.

T.Rich Design is named after it's only employee, Tyler Richards. Tyler was born in Seattle, WA and raised in Provo, UT. He went to High School at Timpview High School in Provo, Utah and then studied Design and Entrepreneurship at Brigham Young University. Tyler found that his passion is to create. Whether it is a new design, website, style, or even a growing business.

T.Rich Design has taken design to the next level, and there is a list of of reputable cliental as proof. T.Rich Design, like Tyler, loves to create. Contact Tyler and T.Rich Design.
